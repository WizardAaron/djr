About Digital Jam Radio

Digital Jam Radio is your gateway to a fresh music experience. Tired of listening to the same old songs? We let you search for tracks, apply audio filters like reverb, bass boost, and echo, and rediscover your favorite music in entirely new ways. Build playlists, save favorites, and make every listening session unique.

Transform Your Music ➜

Apply professional audio filters directly in your browser. Add depth with reverb, pump up the bass, create atmospheric echoes, and more. Our Audio Playground lets you experiment with different effects and find your perfect sound.

Discover & Search ➜

Use our Music Explorer to search for tracks using the Spotify API. Play songs directly in your browser, apply filters on the fly, and add your favorites to custom playlists. Track your listening history and see what resonates with you most.

Features

• Search and play songs with Spotify integration
• Apply real-time audio filters and effects
• Create and manage custom playlists
• Save favorite tracks with localStorage
• Track playback and filter usage analytics
• Responsive design for mobile and desktop
